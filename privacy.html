Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-07

SceneSeed is a free tool for improv teams to collect audience suggestions during a show. This page explains what's collected, what's not, and how it's used. If anything below is unclear, treat the most user-friendly interpretation as the correct one.

What's collected from audience members

  • The text of the suggestion you submit.
  • The timestamp of submission (server time, used to enforce the show's submission window).
  • That's it. No name, no email, no IP address logging, no device fingerprint.

What's collected from hosts (people who sign in)

  • An email address (used to sign in and identify your account).
  • A display name, if you provide one.
  • The shows, groups, and suggestions you create.

Where it's stored

All data is stored on Google Cloud. We do not run our own servers and we do not sell, share, rent, or trade any of this data.

Analytics

The site uses Google Analytics 4 to count page views in aggregate. GA4 may use cookies. You can disable analytics by using a browser extension like uBlock Origin or by enabling "Do Not Track."

Cookies

SceneSeed itself does not set marketing cookies. Sign-in and aggregated analytics may set cookies necessary for those features to work.

Children

SceneSeed is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes meaningfully, the date at the top of this page will be updated and the new version will appear at the same URL.
